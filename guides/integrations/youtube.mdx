title: 'Hansei Integration with YouTube'
description: 'This documentation provides a step-by-step guide on how to seamlessly integrate Hansei with YouTube. Follow these simple instructions to enhance your content management experience.'
---

## Step 1: Log in to Hansei

Navigate to [Hansei's Dashboard](https://hansei.app/dashboard) and Log in to your account.
Once logged in, go to **My Content** to initiate the content addition process.

<img
  src="/images/doc/1.png"
/>

## Step 2: Add Source
Click on **Add New Source** to begin the source addition process.
Select the appropriate file or content source based on your needs.

<img
  src="/images/doc/2.png"
/>

## Step 3: Select Source
Choose the content source from the available options, which include:

-Document
-Text
-URL
-Sitemap
-YouTube
For this integration, select **YouTube** as your content source.

<img
  src="/images/doc/3.png"
/>

## Step 4: Enter YouTube Video URL
Enter the URL of the YouTube video you want to integrate with Hansei.
Click **Upload** to proceed with the integration.

<img
  src="/images/youtube/4.png"
/>

## Step 5: Choose Collection
After entering the YouTube video URL, select a collection under which you want to organize your video.
In this case, we recommend choosing the **Default Collection** for simplicity.

<img
  src="/images/youtube/5.png"
/>

## Step 6: Complete the Upload
Confirm your selection, and the YouTube video will be successfully uploaded to Hansei's Dashboard in your account.

<img
  src="/images/youtube/6.png"
/>

By following these straightforward steps, you can seamlessly integrate YouTube content into Hansei, enhancing your content management capabilities. For additional assistance or information, refer to [Hansei Support](https://hansei.app/contact).
