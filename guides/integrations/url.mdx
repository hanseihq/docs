## Step 1: Login to Hansei
Navigate to [Hansei's Dashboard](https://hansei.app/dashboard) and log in to your account.
Once logged in, go to **My Content** to start adding your content.

<img
  src="/images/doc/1.png"
/>

## Step 2: Add Source
Click on **Add New Source** to initiate the process of adding a new content source.

<img
  src="/images/doc/2.png"
/>

## Step 3: Select Source
Select a content source from the available options: **Document, Text, URL, Sitemap, and YouTube.**
For this demonstration, we'll choose a URL.

<img
  src="/images/doc/3.png"
/>

## Step 4: Enter a URL of Any Website
After selecting **URL**, enter the URL of the website you want to integrate with Hansei.

<img
  src="/images/url/4.png"
/>

## Step 5: Select Collection
Choose a collection under which you want to organize your document. In this case, we'll select **Default Collection**

<img
  src="/images/url/5.png"
/>

## Step 6: Upload URL to Hansei's Dashboard
Confirm the entered URL and collection.
Click on the **Upload** or **Submit** button.

<img
  src="/images/url/6.png"
/>

By following these steps, you can effortlessly integrate any website with Hansei, allowing you to manage and access your content in a centralized location. For more details or assistance, refer to the [Hansei Support](https://hansei.app/contact)
